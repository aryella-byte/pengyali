03

Does Deterrence Work? A Meta-Analysis

Elena Rodriguez, James Park, et al. | Criminology, 2026

📄 Meta-Analysis | 🔑 Criminal Law, Deterrence
Why this matters

Challenges assumptions underlying many criminal justice reforms.

Meta-analysis of 312 studies: certainty shows consistent effects (r=0.34), severity effects negligible (r=0.08). Identifies "tipping point" where excessive severity reduces compliance.
